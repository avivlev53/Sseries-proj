You have reached the series calendar site

By Aviv Levy

On this site you can add series you want to see, delete existing series add comments, mark with a star and delete a specific episode.

The background of the site will change to a picture from the series when today a new episode of the series comes out.

The calendar logic works perfectly so you can change a month back and forth, you can change a year back and forth, you can click on a specific day and the episodes of that day will appear.
